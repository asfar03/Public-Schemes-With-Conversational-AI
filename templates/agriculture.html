Agriculture Schemes | Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers' Welfare

Description: The 'Old Age Pension Scheme under Chief Minister’s Uzhavar Padhukappu Thittam' is a state scheme that was launched by the Social Welfare and Women Empowerment Department, Tamil Nadu

Benefits: The scheme aims to provide a pension of ₹1,000/- to old-age farmers and destitute agricultural laborers who have a membership card under Chief Minister’s Uzhavar Padhukappu Thittam (CMPUT).

  • Pension: ₹1,000/- per month.
  • Free Clothes during Pongal and Deepavali:
    • One free dhoti to a male pensioner.
    • One free saree to a female pensioner.
  • Free Rice in Anganwadi Centres:
    • 2kg/month for those who are taking a nutritious meal.
    • 4kg/month for those who are not taking a nutritious meal.

Description: The 'Chief Minister’s Farmers Security Scheme' is an initiative by the Social Welfare Society of Tamil Nadu, aimed at safeguarding the livelihoods of farmers across the state. It strives to provide comprehensive support and assistance to farmers, recognizing their vital role in the agricultural sector.

  • Pension: Eligible farmers and destitute agricultural laborers receive a monthly pension of ₹1,000/-.
  • Festival Clothing Assistance: During Pongal and Deepavali festivals, male pensioners are provided with a free dhoti, while female pensioners receive a free saree.
  • Food Security: Through this scheme, farmers have access to free rice distribution in Anganwadi Centers, ensuring nutritional support for themselves and their families.

Description: The 'Agricultural Prosperity Initiative' is a pioneering program launched by the Agricultural Development Authority of Tamil Nadu, dedicated to fostering sustainable growth and prosperity in the agricultural sector.

  • Financial Support: Participating farmers are provided with financial assistance to enhance agricultural productivity and invest in modern farming techniques.
  • Expert Guidance: Through specialized training and mentorship programs, farmers receive expert guidance on sustainable farming practices, enabling them to optimize yields while preserving the environment.
  • Market Connectivity: The initiative facilitates market connections, ensuring fair trade and better returns for farmers, thereby promoting economic stability and growth in rural communities.
