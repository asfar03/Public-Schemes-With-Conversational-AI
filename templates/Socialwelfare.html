Social Welfare Schemes | Ministry of Social welfare and Empowerment

Description: The scheme “Scholarship for Transgender Students” was launched by the Social Justice Department, Government of Kerala for providing scholarships to Transgender students from 7th standard onwards studying in State Government/ Government aided/ Self Financing schools.

Benefits: Financial assistance is being provided to Transgender students on the following basis

  • For Transgender students from 7th std. to 10th std.: ₹1000/- per month for 10 months.
  • For Transgender students studying in Plus One & Plus Two: ₹1500/- per month for 10 months.
  • For Transgender students pursuing Diploma, Degree, Post-Graduation, or Professional courses: ₹2000/- per month for 10 months

    • Eligible:

    • Applicant should be Transgender.
    • Applicant should be a resident of Kerala.
    • Applicant should be a student and studying in State Government/ Government aided/ Self Financing schools/colleges.
    • The applicant should be studying in 7th Class to Diploma/Degree/PG Level.
    • There is no income limit.

Description: Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS) is implemented by Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India. It is a non-contributory pension scheme to provide social security to widows of poor family (BPL) of the society.

Benefits: A pension of Rs.300/- per month is provided to Widows between 40 years and 79 years. For persons who are 80 years and above the pension is Rs.500/- per month.

Eligible: The eligibility criteria under Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS) is:-

  • The applicant must be a widow in the age group of 40-79 years.
  • The applicant should belong to a household living below the poverty line according to the criteria prescribed by the Govt. of India

Description: The main objective of the Uttarakhand Pension Yojana is to provide financial assistance to all the needy citizens of the state. Through this scheme, all the eligible citizens of Uttarakhand will be able to maintain themselves properly and their life will also improve. Citizens of Uttarakhand will become self-reliant through Uttarakhand Pension Yojana. All the citizens coming below the poverty line can take advantage of the Uttarakhand Pension Yojana.

Benefits:

  • This financial assistance is Rs 14,400 per year.

Eligibility:

  • The applicant must be a permanent resident of Uttarakhand.
  • The applicant's age should be more than 60 years.
  • The applicant should not get pension benefits from any other sources.
